About Me

I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow (STEM Talent Fellow) at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST). I earned my Ph.D. in Systems Engineering and Engineering Management from The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK) in 2024. I also hold a master's degree in Electrical Engineering from the University of Alberta and a bachelor's degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My research spans graph algorithms, social network analysis (e.g., influence maximization), and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG).
